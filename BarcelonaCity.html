Barcelona - city of dreams

Barcelona - beautiful, enchanting, spectacular and very unusual capital of Catalonia, the main autonomous region of the Spanish kingdom. The second largest city of the Iberian Peninsula, one of the largest ports in the Mediterranean and the world, a favorite point of attraction for travelers of all stripes. Barcelona is so diverse and vibrant that it can rightfully be called one of the most beautiful cities on earth!

Main attractions of Barcelona

Barcelona is one of the most beautiful and ancient cities in Europe. Many sights of Barcelona are known all over the world.
